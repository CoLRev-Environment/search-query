**Error Code**: F2012

**Message**: ``A search for publication years must include at least another search term.``

**Scope**: PLATFORM.WOS

**Problematic query**:

.. code-block:: text

    PY=2000

**Recommended query**:

.. code-block:: text

    TI=term AND PY=2000

**Typical fix**: Combine the year filter with at least one other search term.
