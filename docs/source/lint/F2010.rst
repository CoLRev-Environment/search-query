===========================

**Error Code**: F2010

**Message**: ``Year span must be five or less.``

**Scope**: PLATFORM.WOS

**Problematic query**:

.. code-block:: text

    A AND PY=2000-2020

**Recommended query**:

.. code-block:: text

    A AND PY=2015-2020

**Typical fix**: The parser automatically adjusts the year span to 5 years.
