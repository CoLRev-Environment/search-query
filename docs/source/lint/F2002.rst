========================

**Error Code**: F2002

**Message**: ``Wildcard characters (*, ?, $) not supported in year search.``

**Scope**: PLATFORM.WOS

**Problematic query**:

.. code-block:: text

    dHealth[ti] AND 200*[dp]

**Recommended query**:

.. code-block:: text

    dHealth[ti] AND 2000:2010[dp]

**Typical fix**: Replace with year range.
