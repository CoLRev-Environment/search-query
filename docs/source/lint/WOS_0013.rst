WOS_0013 — invalid-near-query
=============================

**Error Code**: WOS_0013

**Message**: ``NEAR operator applied to AND query.``

**Problematic query**:

.. code-block:: text

    # PLATFORM.WOS
    "TI=((A AND B) NEAR/3 C)"

**Recommended query**:

.. code-block:: text

    # PLATFORM.WOS
    "TI=((A NEAR/3 C) AND (B NEAR/3 C))"
