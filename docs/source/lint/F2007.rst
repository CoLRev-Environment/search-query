**Error Code**: F2007

**Message**: ``NEAR distance is too large (max: 15).``

**Scope**: PLATFORM.WOS

**Problematic query**:

    TI=term1 NEAR/20 term2

**Recommended query**:

**Typical fix**: Reduce the NEAR distance to 15 or less.
