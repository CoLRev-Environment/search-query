**Error Code**: F2004

**Message**: ``Left-hand wildcard must be preceded by at least three characters.``

**Scope**: PLATFORM.WOS

**Typical fix**: Ensure the term before a left-hand wildcard (*) has at least three characters.

**Problematic query**:

.. code-block:: text

    TI=*te

**Correct query**:

.. code-block:: text

    TI=abc*te
